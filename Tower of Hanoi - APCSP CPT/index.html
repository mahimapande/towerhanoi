GOAL OF THE GAME: MOVE all 3 disks from tower 1 to tower 3 within 7 maximum moves !

In order to move a disk, click on the particular disk and the respective tower button to move it there

You are only allowed to move ONE DISK at a time

You CANNOT place a big disk on a small disk. It will be counted as invalid !

ALL disks, expect the one disk being moved, must be on a tower
